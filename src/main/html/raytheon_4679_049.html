Rewards

  • 401(k) / Retirement Plans

    Watch your financial wellness grow. Enrollment eligibility begins on your first day of work with an employer match up to 3%.

  • Performance Recognition

    Be recognized and recognize others with our new employee awards program. At Raytheon, we appreciate notable and exceptional performance.

  • Work / Life Balance

    Raytheon appreciates work/life balance. Employees can work 9 hours a day every week and receive every other Friday off*
    * Requires departmental/manager approval; Interns/Co-ops in California may not be eligible to participate.

  • Student Loan Repayment Contribution

    Through the 401(k), called the Raytheon Savings and Investment Plan, the company will make a dollar for dollar contribution for amounts employees spend repaying their student loans, up to their maximum match percentage.

  • Educational Assistance

    Our educational assistance program provides reimbursement for approved courses and accredited certificate/degree programs relevant to your position. Full-time employees are eligible for up to $10,000 in assistance each year.

  • Paid Time Off

    Accrue paid time off monthly. Receive 12 holidays per year including the week between Christmas and New Year’s Day.

  • Relocation Assistance

    Select technical positions are eligible for relocation packages. See specific job description for eligibility requirements.

  • Parental Leave

    Raytheon offers all eligible employees up to three weeks paid parental leave for the birth or adoption of a child (separate from paid maternity leave or Family & Medical Leave Act).

Responsibilities

National Defense, Cloud Technologies, Agile Development, DevOps, Big Data, Teamwork … Opportunities at Raytheon span the full product development lifecycle, including new capability development as well as sustainment and enhancement of existing deployed systems.

Raytheon Intelligence, Information and Services (IIS) in State College, PA is seeking talented Systems-Software Engineers to support classified programs that develop and enhance integrated sensor, signal processing, complex analytics, and big data systems for Intelligence Community customers. The successful candidate has excellent written and verbal communication skills, works effectively in a team environment, and is flexible & adaptable to respond to customer mission needs. Come join Raytheon's team of engineers solving problems that make the world a safer place and help to secure our great nation! Come support those who provide us with all the freedoms we enjoy. Click here to view a brief video and learn more about Raytheon: https://youtu.be/8B-k34faFAE.

About State College, PA
State College is a college town, dominated economically and demographically by the presence of the University Park main campus of The Pennsylvania State University, one of the ten largest public universities in the United States. State College provides access to many cultural and athletic activities & events, and yet is only minutes away from rural Pennsylvania country-side. Many Raytheon State College employees enjoy a commute of 15 minutes or less! Click here to view a brief video and learn more about Raytheon State College: https://youtu.be/f299m09q0Fs

Compensation & Benefits
While benefits may vary based on business unit, location and/or union status, Raytheon employees receive comprehensive benefits as part of a competitive compensation package. Health and wellness plans are available for you and your eligible children. Other benefits include insurance, education assistance, income protection, investment/retirement and work/life programs.

Clearance Requirements
This position requires eligibility to obtain a TS/SCI security clearance. A current, active TS/SCI or DoD-TS security clearance is highly preferred, and includes a sign-on bonus. U.S. citizenship is required.

Required Skills:
This is an entry-level systems-software engineering position. Recent or upcoming college graduates are welcome to apply.
  • Ability to work effectively on a team of software & systems engineers
  • Ability to generate technical documentation
  • Ability to conduct technical research, document and present results
  • Strong interpersonal, communications, organizational, and planning skills
  • Strong commitment to quality customer service and on-schedule task completions
  • Ability to manage time and resources to meet deadlines
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office

Desired Skills:
  • Active TS/SCI or DoD-TS security clearance
  • Coursework or experience in systems engineering
  • Coursework or experience in software engineering
  • Coursework or experience with Agile Development & Continuous Integration
  • Coursework or experience with cloud technologies, including Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Direct experience in software development on Linux and/or Windows platforms
  • Previous internship experience

Education Requirements:
This position requires a 4-year bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM).



This position requires either a U.S. Person or a Non-U.S. Person who is eligible to obtain any required Export Authorization. 151291
