Client Partnerships Manager-Self Service-(0104687)
Do you want to work in a fun, smart, and energetic environment where you will be challenged, rewarded AND inspired? Come work with top performers at CJ Affiliate to make a real difference in how top brands run their businesses. We are the worlds leading affiliate marketing network - we transform digital marketing with the help of big data and business intelligence. We help advertisers drive more sales and expand their reach and we want to hear from you!
As a Client Partnerships Manager for Self-service, you will provide self-service clients with expert advice on affiliate marketing best practices and growth opportunities; as well as, contribute to the creation of new content, scalable tools, and shared projects. Client Partnerships Managers play a key role in training and building relationships with CJs clients, identifying areas of opportunities, and working with counterparts in other CJ functions to ensure client needs are met.
Do these things interest you? You will:
  • Support your Sr. Client Development Manager in positioning CJ Affiliate as the go-to self-service platform for affiliate marketing
  • Execute on quarterly and annual plans to meet key business objectives
  • Assist in developing and sharing content to be used by clients, including creation of customer surveys, webinars, videos, blog posts, and newsletters.
  • Consult with clients on the state of their program and sharing growth opportunities and optimization strategies
  • Collaborating with other departments in CJ (Product, Engineering, Publisher Development, Data Science, Technical Support, Finance, Legal, Marketing, Business Systems, Advertiser Operations) and advocating for the self-service perspective.
  • Work with CJ Support team and clients to troubleshoot complex account and technical related items.
  • Gather client feedback and share internally to shape future developments.
  • Support initiatives to grow client revenue and drive product adoption through research, data analysis and idea creation.

What we look for:
  • Bachelor's Degree and minimum of 2 years experience in the online marketing industry-ideally digital performance marketing.
  • Clear and effective communication and can convey complex results in a simple, clear manner, using solid business acumen, demonstrating a competent level command of digital marketing concepts.
  • Ability to influence/develop account strategies that leverage analysis and insights to contribute to meeting or exceeding advertiser objectives.
  • Ability to execute a variety of tasks within designated time frames, delivering exceptional quality results.
  • Results oriented problem solver.
  • Proficient knowledge of the Microsoft suite of products including Windows, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Experience in working with remote teams is a plus.
  • SaaS experience is a plus.
  • Familiarity with Pendo, Pardot, and Salesforce is a plus.
