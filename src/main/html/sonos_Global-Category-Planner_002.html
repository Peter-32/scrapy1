Global Category Planner

Department: Business Operations

Location: Santa Barbara

Req#: 5672

Sonos is a fast growing global enterprise and an established brand that combines innovation with lifestyle, 
with huge momentum on its journey to a simple, but far reaching vision – to fill every home with music.  

In line with this ambition and the resulting growth plans, there is a continuous need for Sonos to invest in its organization across the globe, thus growing the level of professionalism, talent and experience within the Company. 

At Sonos, the Global Operations team is responsible for all global Supply Chain, Customer Service and Business Operations activities required to serve to its global sales channels and end user customers within best in class product delivery and after sales service levels and against lowest possible levels of cost and inventory.

Within Global Operations, the Global Planning team has responsibility for the End to End balancing of Consumer and Channel Partner Demand with Supply.    This includes the responsibility to secure product availability against agreed Service Levels, through the careful management of Product Supply plans, as well as through the maintenance of overall Sonos and Sales Channel Inventory levels against agreed business targets.  This position also includes responsibility for coordinating new product launch activities, end of life production plans and channel transitions.

Aligned to its vision to listen better and its culture of continuous innovation, Sonos is investing significantly in the expansion of its product portfolio. This results in a continuous flow of new product introductions as well as product refresh, upgrade and transition projects. The need to manage these projects effectively and efficiently adds significant responsibility and complexity to the activities of the Global Planning team.

Working closely with Sales, Product Marketing, Product Management, Customer Services and the Supply Chain Operations team, the Global Category Planner is the central point of coordination for the end to end planning of Demand, Supply and Inventory for a specific product category of the Sonos product portfolio.

What You’ll Do
  • Working closely with Product Marketing, Sales and the Supply Chain teams, the Global Category Planner is responsible to develop and maintain end to end product plans across the product lifecycles of his/her product category, containing long term sourcing and production capacity requirements, inventory and sales projections.
  • Working closely together with the Regional Demand Planners as well as the Supply Planning team, the Category Planner actively supports the weekly & monthly Supply & Demand Balancing cycle and translation of Sales Forecasts into clear supply plans, material sourcing plans, inventory projections, product availability service levels and allocations, for his/her product category.
  • The Category Planner has responsibility for managing the alignment of Demand and Supply, including projected finished goods inventory, and all analytics and reporting relevant to their product category in the Anaplan system.
  • As member of the Integrated Business Planning organization and Global Planning team, the Global Category Planner
    • Conducts various analysis both on own initiative and on request from the wider Global Operations organization to support decision making around product category related topics.
    • Takes a leadership role in the development and implementation of a best in class planning infrastructure of business processes, operating practices and tools.
    • Generates relevant business scenarios and risk mitigation plans for their assigned product category, and presents recommended supply/demand action plans to drive the monthly Sales & Operations Planning Decision cycle.
  • Working in concert with the product development program teams, the Global Category Planner is responsible for managing the transition of New Products from the ramp phase to launch execution and sustaining mode. 
  • The Global Category Planner orchestrates End of Life planning activities, including EOP allocation setting, facilitating EOP decision process, EOP planning, managing inventory transition plans of both Components and Finished Good products across the Sonos Supply Chain and the global Sonos Sales channels.
