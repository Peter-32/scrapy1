The Inogen mission is to improve freedom and independence for oxygen therapy patients through innovative products and services. Inogen is a manufacturer and Accredited Homecare Provider.

Inogen is focused on quality care, continuous improvement, and outstanding customer satisfaction. We are a stable, USAbased company dedicated to providing an exciting and rewarding career to our employees. Our work environment is casual yet highperforming, and we are looking for seriously talented, motivated, and fast-paced professionals to join our team.

JOB DESCRIPTION The role of a Reimbursement Denials Advocate is key to optimizing cash collection.  By diligently working claim denials, research, and submitting appeal packages with the highest level of accuracy, the successful candidate will achieve their goals as well as contribute to the team's goals.

Our Billing team is focused on continuous improvement so we welcome deep thinkers who analyze the root cause and recommend corrective action.

Are you a go-getter, energetic, and persuasive individual that enjoys problem solving?  Have you spent 1-3 years in the healthcare billing arena?  If that is you, let us know!

Key Competencies:

  • Detail-oriented with focus on accuracy.
  • Analytical and able to find root cause
  • Ability to multitask with software – manage multiple systems including CRM, Fastrack, Endeavor, MVP, Paypal and Call on Demand phone system in a fast-paced environment
    • Successful and timely submission of appeals and other insurance claims
    • Demonstrated ability to consulting with physicians and hospital referral sources is preferred

Qualifications:

  • 1 year of billing experience required
  • 3 years of Medicare and insurance billing preferred in Oxygen or HME/DME
  • 1-3 years appeals experience a plus
  • A complete understanding of Medicare program rules is preferred
  • Experience with Fastrack, BrightTree, Oracle, and/or Excel preferred
  • Bachelor's degree preferred
