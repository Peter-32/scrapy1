Sr. Industrial Designer, Packaging (Experience & Visual Design)

Department: Design and Creative

Location: Santa Barbara

Req#: 5455

Sonos is seeking a senior level Designer to join its high-performing, collaborative Packaging Experience & Visual Design team. The role of a Senior Designer in the Packaging Design team at Sonos requires balancing a deep sense of collaboration & communication, with an eye for design details, quality, and a desire to deliver the ‘right product’. Designers at Sonos are expected to embrace the design team behaviors of Trust, Debate, and Accountability. The ability to envision and shape Sonos design experiences for premium packaging for today and beyond is your mission. This approach requires attention to detail and craft that is fueled by deep curiosity, respecting the challenges of delivering a premium ‘out of box experience’ for iconic products.
About You

More than a candidate that checks every box, we’re looking for people who are excited to work, learn, and grow at Sonos—no matter their background or how they identify. If that’s you, we hope you’ll apply for this role.

You want to be part of a team.

You come with new ideas and a unique point of view. You look forward to collaborating with a diverse team of individuals. You assume everyone’s best intentions, welcome a healthy debate, and embrace differing opinions. You eagerly seek and give help. Transparency tops your list of values, and you proactively contribute to a culture of respect and inclusion.

You enjoy a challenge.

Inquisitive and focused, you see every challenge as an opportunity. You’re ambitious and comfortable making mistakes because you learn from them and bounce back quickly. You would rather create the future than wait for it. You prioritize long-term value over short-term objectives.

You love to listen.

You approach every interaction with curiosity and a desire to understand. You want to make a positive impact in the world. You’re passionate about culture and know the power that music, film, podcasts, games, and stories have to bring people together.

What You’ll Do
As a designer in Packaging Design, you are expected to collaborate with design and engineering partners on programs to deliver premium, sustainable package designs. Your role fundamentally provides 2D and 3D expertise to help build, refine, and communicate great design solutions that uphold or improve the packaging experience. Your role is not limited to this -- you should also be ready to contribute to early concept exploration, visualization, and presentations to visually articulate the packaging design intent. Packaging Design is committed to delivering an easy, enjoyable opening experience for our customers.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Apply your 2D and 3D expertise and skills to help create, refine, and communicate superior structural design intent that uphold or improve the packaging experience platform.
  • Create volume models, structural prototypes, dielines and renders to communicate Sonos’ 
  • point of view on unboxing experiences.
  • Experience with various packaging substrates such as SBS, Corrugate, Rigid box
  • Experience with packaging manufacturing and print technologies
  • Provide clear, focused direction (i.e. design brief, sketches) to drive programs and initiatives 
  • Further develop our sustainable packaging exploration and initiatives to deliver on our Sustainability KPIs

Success in this role requires: 
  • Aesthetic sensitivity: Ability to consistently deliver and innovate design concepts that express a strong eye towards form and function, attention to detail and conceptual clarity. Candidates must demonstrate the ability to draw upon contemporary trends in a way that results in aesthetic unboxing experiences for the holistic packaging system (exterior and interior). 
  • Creative thinking: Ability to think creatively and critically across a range of problem types while keeping sight of the “big picture” goals. Additionally, the right candidate will have a genuine interest in working across problems that touch upon industrial design, packaging, interaction design and brand in a manner that reinforces the Sonos design and brand promise. 
  • Organizational skills: Candidates should be detail-driven and seasoned in navigating packaging interaction and development processes for volume production: tracking schedules, program aims and manufacturing considerations while striving to deliver compelling packaging experiences that support premium products. 

Skills You’ll Need
Basic Qualifications:
  • BA or BS in the field of Industrial Design, Packaging Engineering or other related Bachelor's Degree 
  • A minimum of 4-7+ years experience in designing premium retail packaging in a corporate or 
  • consulting team environment
  • Experience in delivering premium packaging solutions for worldwide markets with a focus in the areas of consumer electronics, domestic appliances, beauty, or other related CPG industries
  • Proven ability to develop & resolve design concepts through sketches, mock-ups, & models; from 2D to full 3D prototypes
  • Proven ability in developing packaging structures for volume production and manufacturing 
  • Experience interacting and effectively communicating with internal partners & 3rd party vendors throughout the design development process
  • Proficiency with software such as Adobe CC, G-Suite or MS Office, Keyshot, Artios, Solidworks, Siemens NX, or similar
  • Able to travel approximately 10-15% annually (nationally and internationally)
  • Must have a portfolio available for viewing.

Preferred Qualifications:
  • Understanding of impacts of packaging materials and design solutions on Colors, Materials and Finish for product
  • Ability to maintain aesthetic sensitivity from concept to manufacture without losing the spirit of the original packaging design intent
  • Ability to multitask, work quickly, accurately, but take time for reflection and innovation 
  • Demonstrated ability to translate trends and culture into compelling products.
  • Strong command of American-English is critical, a second spoken language is a plus
More About Sonos
