Marketing Data Analyst – Communications

Apply Requisition ID 151876BR Date updated 11/14/2019
.
What's YOUR Mission?
Here at Integrated Defense Systems (IDS), we work together as one global team creating trusted, innovative solutions to make the world a safer place. Our innovation spans all domains: from land and sea to air, space and cyberspace. We’re inspired by a noble mission that’s shared by IDS employees around the globe and an inclusive culture that empowers employees and celebrates their contributions.

What We Offer: Whether you’re just starting out on your career journey or are an experienced professional, IDS offers a robust total rewards package that goes above and beyond with compensation; healthcare, wellness, retirement and work/life benefits; career development and recognition programs. Some of the superior benefits IDS offers include paternal leave, flexible work schedules, achievement awards, educational assistance and child/adult backup care.


Job Description
Are you passionate about data? Do you enjoy working in collaborative and high energy environments? Are you the type of person that enjoys continuous learning and cross-functional collaboration? We seek an entrepreneurial data analyst to join our integrated communications team. Advising multiple internal and external campaign teams, you will provide analysis and insights that are instrumental to targeting audiences, developing and refining channel strategies and optimizing content. Leveraging a suite of analytics and reporting tools, you will help address the complex challenges of marketing effectiveness, ROI, audience behavior and campaign effectiveness while working closely with channel owners and campaign leads to monitor and analyze campaign outcomes. Additionally, you will serve on a cross-functional, cross-business, team of analysts charged with collaborating on the creation of new innovative solutions for your customers.

Key Responsibilities will include:

Data-Driven Counsel – provide data-driven counsel to improve effectiveness of employee engagement and external marketing campaigns.

Audience Analysis – Create and maintain in-depth profiles on campaign audiences and their influencers. Develop targeting recommendations based on audience research.

Analytics Reporting – Develop standardized reporting to monitor and analyze channel effectiveness, audience sentiment and campaign return on investment (ROI).

Channel Optimization – Collaborating with campaign channel leads, use data to refine channel strategies, test messaging and optimize marketing spend.

Data & Reporting Tools Management – Lead department understanding, use and integration of data analysis, targeting and reporting tools. Monitor data science trends, emerging tools and technologies to continually improve frameworks, processes and best practices.

Competitive Research – Monitors competitor tactics and industry best practices to inform campaign strategies.

Required Qualifications:

  • Minimum 2 years of experience in data analysis or marketing research.
  • Ability to create reports, templates and repeatable processes.
  • Proficiency in Google Analytics, social media analytics, Excel and Microsoft Office suite
  • US Citizenship status is required as this position may require the ability to access US only data systems.

Desired Skills

  • Excellent communication skills, with ability to process and present complex information in a concise and compelling manner to non-technical audiences
  • Ability and eagerness to learn, educate and mentor others
  • Experience in diverse domains (marketing, communications, information technology, business development, human resources)
  • Experience with Sprinklr, Datorama, Cision, BrightEdge and/or Quid
  • Must be able to work both independently and as a member of a multi-functional team
  • Defense industry experience a plus

Required Education:

Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Econometrics, Engineering, Computer Science, Finance, Mathematics or a quantitative degree in other social science or hard science fields


This position can be a Salary Grade G07 or Salary Grade G08 based on the candidate's qualifications as they relate to the skills, experience and responsibility requirements for the position.

Business Unit Profile
Headquartered in Tewksbury, Massachusetts, IDS has 32 locations around the world. Its broad portfolio of weapons, sensors and integration systems supports its customer base across multiple mission areas, including air and missile defense systems; missile defense radars; early warning radars; naval ship operating systems; C5ITM products and services; and other advanced technologies. IDS provides affordable, integrated solutions to a broad international and domestic customer base, including the U.S. Missile Defense Agency, the U.S. Armed Forces and the Department of Homeland Security.
