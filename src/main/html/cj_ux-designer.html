UX Designer - (0104925)
 

Location:

Westlake Village or Santa Barbara, CA or Chicago, Ill or Westborough, MA




What will you impact:  


As a CJ Affiliate UX Designer, you will be responsible for helping research, design, and develop new features for our industry leading product. We use lean UX validation techniques, customer interviews, and analytics to help us validate our product direction. We are looking for individuals who can help grow our team and product and share their experience in creating great software. 

If you love problem-solving, working in a fast-paced environment, collaborating directly with highly engaged clients and desire to have a direct impact on an organization, this is the opportunity or you! CJs Product & UX Team is a diverse group of highly talented and dedicated individuals that love building products that make it easier for our clients to manage their performance marketing efforts.  
 
Learn more about the solutions we have delivered here: https://junction.cj.com/article/tag/menu-cj-solutions 


A Day in the Life: 

  • Contribute to the design of useful, usable, and desirable products in a team environment. 
  • Designing the information architecture and user-experiences from low-fidelity sketches to hi-fidelity pixel perfect mockups and interactive prototypes.  
  • Collaborating heavily with the product team throughout the duration of the project 
  • Creating rapid prototypes to validate design concepts with stakeholders and customers 
  • Facilitate the design process, present and iterate design solutions 
  • Collaborating and communicating professionally with clients and internal teams 
  • Staying abreast of UX trends and looking for creative ideas and inspiration in parallel analogous worlds 
  • Pairing with developers to bring designs to life 
  • Supporting pattern library and component development 
  • Designing for accessibility and responsiveness 
 

We use InVision, Sketch, and Pendo. We have a distributed team, so you need to be comfortable with working independently. You will report to the UX Manager. 

 
Qualifications and skills, we are looking for: 
  • 5+ years of industry experience as a user experience designer, interaction designer, information architect, or similar design-related role. 
  • Fluency in best practices for information architecture and interaction design, user-centered design process, as well as strong knowledge of user experience principles and techniques 
  • 4+ years of experience working in direct collaboration with product managers, developers, and/or stakeholders. 
  • 4+ years of experience creating prototypes or wire-frames for complex web applications and/or workflows. 
  • Familiarity with Javascript frameworks (React), HTML, CSS 
  • An online portfolio demonstrating samples of design deliverables (personas, journey maps, wireframes, and prototypes) is required to apply for this role. 
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies 
  • Bachelor’s degree
