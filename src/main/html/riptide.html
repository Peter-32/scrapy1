BMS Software Technician

What You’ll Do:

A BMS/BAS Software Technician works directly with the Riptide project team. Activities will include project configuration, programming, startup, troubleshooting, commissioning and service. Hands-on activity may involve minor wiring (low-voltage) and panel fabrication.

We are looking for individuals who love a start-up pace, can wear multiple hats and are self-starters. If you like seeing your hard work provide high value results for customers, join us at Riptide.

Functions and Responsibilities include the following:

  • Manage multiple projects and communicating status and/or risks to project team.
  • Building systems integration and configuration.
  • Commissioning, graphics, programming, test and startup.
  • First level support including investigating issues and implementing corrective action.

Technical Skills:

  • Basic knowledge of BACnet and Modbus communication protocols.
  • Basic knowledge of control programming and graphics for control systems.
  • Basic knowledge of field wiring and terminations.
  • Basic knowledge of HVAC systems and temperature controls (DDC).

Qualifications:

  • Familiarity with the concepts of new construction, retrofit and service projects.
  • Knowledge of the principles of heating and air conditioning and major components.
  • Understanding of the basics of HVAC controls (DDC)
  • Strong troubleshooting skills.
  • Understanding of electricity and electrical principles.

Experience:

  • HVAC installation & service: 2 years (Required)
  • BMS/BAS/DDC controls programming: 1 year (Preferred)

Education: Associate (Preferred)

Job Type: Full-time

Location: Santa Barbara, CA (Required)

Sales Engineer West Coast, Central and East Coast

Responsibilities

  • Communicate with customers to solve sales, technical and application matters related to building controls, energy, HVAC and enterprise facility management
  • Anticipate and respond to customers to ensure overall customer satisfaction with Riptide’s product and service offerings
  • Build and foster relationships with strategic partners
  • Generate proposals from a range of work documents
  • Accompany sales team on technical visits to end-user and partner sites
  • Create technical sales and training presentations
  • Work with HVAC partners to develop controls strategies and KPI’s
  • Manage pilot projects for new customers, and refine the process as you go – creating a best practices guide for future pilots
  • Pilot project management includes working with the end-user to identify pilot locations; working with the end-user to determine success plan for pilots; working with internal services group to hire installation contractors; manage installation and start-up of the pilot project; training contractors and end-users; and periodically reporting the status of the installed project to the end-user as well as tracking project success

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering (or equivalent) and preferably a minimum of 1-3 year experience in a similar role or 5+ year experience in building controls, HVAC
  • Proficiency with PowerPoint, Word, and Excel
  • IT savvy; networking experience a plus
  • Comfortable building documents using Visio or similar vector graphing tools
  • Ability to read blueprints and complete on-site surveys
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to problem solve and succeed in unstructured environment
  • Fearless, self-reliant, and highly self-motivated personality with great time management skills and a focus on consistent execution

Interns

Looking for engineering or business-focused college students for internships based in Santa Barbara, CA. Depending on your skill set, you will help the executive and product team on projects that drive our growth.

Mechanical Engineering Majors: Students with HVAC courses complete and who have an interest in smart buildings. Best candidates have a background in sales engineering roles or ability to develop technical materials.

Marketing or Business Related Majors: Students with marketing, economics, and journalism who can help apply their skills in these areas: digital marketing, customer relationship management, financial forecasting and/or writing.

Gain experience working with a fast-paced startup, where you can see different functional areas of a business to help you find your area of interest in your future career. For rising seniors, this could lead to a full-time offer post graduation.
